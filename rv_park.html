RV Park

The RV Park is conveniently located behind the Knights Inn and is owned and operated by the hotel

 

Full RV Hook-up

Our RV Park offers campers full hook-up with both 30 amp and 50 amp hookup. In addition, campers have access to the hotel amenities:

  • Cable TV hook-up - with Free HBO
  • Telephone hookup
  • Outdoor Swimming Pool
  • Picnic Tables
  • Barbecue Grill
  • Guest Laundry
  • High-speed Wireless Internet
  • Ice-Machines and Vending Machines

Please call the hotel directly to check for availability and make reservations.
