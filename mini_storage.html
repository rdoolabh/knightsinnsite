Mini Storage Units

We offer three different styles of mini-storage units:

  • 5 feet x 5 feet
  • 5 feet x 10 feet
  • 10 feet x 10 feet



We do not require new customers to pay a deposit. However, we do ask that new customers pay for the remainder of the current month and the following month.

Security Locks are available for sale in the office and are recommended.

Please call the hotel directly to check for availability.
