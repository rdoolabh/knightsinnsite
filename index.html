Welcome to The Knights Inn - Beebe!

Expect value and friendly service in the heart of Arkansas when you stay at Knights Inn Beebe hotel. Located just 35 miles from Little Rock and the Clinton Presidential Library, our Beebe hotel is right off Highways 67/167 and 64, offering easy access to popular capital-area attractions.

From in-room microwaves and refrigerators to guest laundry facilities, our hotel offers all the amenities to revitalize your travels. Come and enjoy our clean and comfortable rooms before diving into our invigorating outdoor pool. Free Wi-Fi Internet access means you can stay connected to friends and family from the comfort of your room. Handicapped-accessible and non-smoking rooms are available, and kids 17 and under stay free with an adult.
